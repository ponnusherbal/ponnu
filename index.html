PONNU'S AYURVEDA

Ayurveda is a traditional Indian system of medicine that has been in practice for over 5,000 years. It is based on the idea of balancing the body, mind, and spirit through natural remedies, lifestyle changes, and a healthy diet. Ayurveda is gaining popularity as people seek alternatives to Western medicine, and research is beginning to show the health benefits of this ancient practice.
